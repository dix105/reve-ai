Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Reve image generator?

Reve is an AI image generation model made for artwork image creation. It turns text prompts or photos into creative, stylized images with a soft and expressive visual style.

What type of images does Reve generate best?

Reve is best suited for stylized and dream-inspired images. It works well for illustrations, creative concepts, mood visuals, and expressive designs instead of realistic photos.

Can I generate images using text prompts?

Yes. You can write a text prompt describing what you want, and the Reve model will generate an artwork images based on your description.

Can I use my own photo with the Reve model?

Yes. You can upload a photo and apply the Reve image style to it. The model transforms the photo into a creative and artistic version instead of a realistic edit.

How long does image generation take?

Image generation usually takes few moments. The exact time may vary slightly depending on image size and system load.

Is Reve suitable for realistic image generation?

Reve is designed for creative visuals. If you need highly realistic or photo-accurate images, a different AI model may be more suitable.

Are my prompts and images kept private?

Yes. Your uploaded images and prompts are processed securely and are not shared with others. They are removed after a short period.

What image formats does Reve support?

Reve supports common image formats such as JPG, PNG, and WEBP for both uploads and downloads.
